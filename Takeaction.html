What Can You Do?

As an individual, you have the power to make a difference in promoting clean and affordable energy. Here are some steps you can take:

  • Switch to energy-efficient appliances and light bulbs.
  • Consider renewable energy sources for your home, like solar panels.
  • Reduce your energy consumption by unplugging devices when not in use.
  • Support policies and initiatives that promote sustainable energy practices.
  • Educate others about the benefits of clean energy.

NGOs Working on This

Several non-governmental organizations (NGOs) are dedicated to promoting clean and sustainable energy. Here are some you can support:

  • Greenpeace: Advocates for renewable energy and fights against climate change. Visit Website
  • The Sierra Club: Focuses on clean energy solutions and environmental conservation. Visit Website
